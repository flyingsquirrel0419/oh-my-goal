# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, harassment-free, and focused on constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation details, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make contributors feel unsafe.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants when behavior violates this code.

Report concerns through the security/contact channels listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
